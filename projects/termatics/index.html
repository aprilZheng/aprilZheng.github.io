Termatics Projects

During my time at Termatics, I worked on two machine learning projects that focused on bridging RGB and thermal imagery. These projects aimed to explore the potential of combining computer vision techniques with 3D modeling and thermal image prediction. While the results were mixed, they provided valuable insights and a strong foundation for future improvements.

2D to 3D

Using pairs of 2D RGB and 2D thermal images, this project aimed to reconstruct a 3D thermal model. Despite challenges in achieving accurate thermal representation, this work highlighted the complexities of 3D reconstruction with thermal data and the importance of data quality and modeling techniques.

RGB to Thermal

This project focused on training mahcine learning models to predict corresponding thermal images from RGB inputs. The results showed promising initial outputs, but there remains room for refinement in model accuracy and generalizability.
